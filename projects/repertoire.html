Repertoire is a catalogue; a range of regularly performed or exhibited skills, techniques and abilities. – Oxford English Dictionary

Repertoire is this website. It is a collection of my work, skills, experiences and the people I have worked alongside. It is also a witness to my never-ending cycles of learning and creating with curiosity. Repertoire started as a basic wireframe sketch, then a Figma prototype before evolving into what you now interact with. It all started with a determination to code up a website from scratch and I did with help from many supportive sources.

Repertoire is living and this is only its first appearance. I intend for it to grow as I grow in my journey. Now, please sit back and navigate your way through Repertoire.
