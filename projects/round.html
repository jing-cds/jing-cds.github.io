works | materials, product | round

round is a set of dainty ceramic tea cups inspired by Chinese tea culture. Unlike the classic white and blue colour palette, round is strikingly bold with specks of gold. Half are an inverted hourglass with widen centre, while the other half open up like tulips. Whoever you hold, they sit snugly between your fingers as you sip, filling the air with a calming, subtle tea aroma.
