works | installation, materials | mycelium

Please note that this is still an ongoing research project at the time of writing in Mar 2025. Specific design decisions and details are omitted, feel free to reach out to me (contact page!) to learn more about the project.


Corpus Rhizoma is a research project that aims to reveal the omnipresence of fungi and their entanglement with the world. It is an installation made of mycelium, the root structure of mushroom, with interactive augmented reality.


The installation has an experimental approach to bring attention to the concept of human body-(non)human relationships in collaboration with augmented reality. In part of the installation's preparation, I was responsible for creating mycelium artefacts and co-developing the mycelium cultivating process with Gyungju to achieve the desirable aesthetics.
