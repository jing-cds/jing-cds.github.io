works | material, product | Book: idiosyncrasy

The book, created for its subject in unconventional publishing, has to be to the theme of unconventionality or in this case, it achieved idiosyncrasy with its uncommon tools. In short, the book is really more of a toolbox but less bulky in its A6 form.

The book, like all books, has sections but no chapter titles. Rather, it relies on the reader’s close attention to find how they are loosely associated; whether visible, intangible or conceptual. In all, the book explored dimensionality and toolmaking, challenging the conventional use of materials. The photographs show a spread of each non-existent chapter.
